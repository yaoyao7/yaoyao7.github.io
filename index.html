I am a fifth-year Ph.D. student at the University of Iowa. My advisor is Qihang Lin, and I am coadvised by Tianbao Yang at TAMU. Before joining UIowa, I was a risk analyst in Transamerica. Previously, I obtained my M.S. in Mathematical Finance from Rutgers University and B.Eng. and B.Econ. from Shanghai University.

My research interests include continuous optimization and machine learning, especially DC problems and constrained optimization.
